Kwashiorkor

Malnutrition produced by a severely inadequate amount of protein in the diet.

Protein malnutrition, or kwashiorkor, is mostly found in people living in geographical areas that have limited food resources. It's most commonly seen in children whose diets are low in protein and calories. Delayed growth in children, a swollen stomach and frequent infections are symptoms. Treatment for kwashiorkor may include a slow increase in calories followed by an increase in protein.
